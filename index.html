WouldYa

A discord bot for the popular game "Would You Rather"

Questions

/question

There are more than 3000 unique questions! And more everyday! "Would you rather electricity or water?" "Would you rather clean your own home or hire someone to do it for you?" "Would you rather have no emotions or be able to deeply feel every little thing?"

Interactions

Boost your server interaction and activity.
Talk about questions and choises with your friends and vote on every question. Voting is global across all servers. You may choose to make your account private.

Suggestions

/suggest

Get involved in the community by suggesting questions.
You will be notified if your question is accepted or denied. Suggesting troll questions will limit your access to the bot.

Stats

/stats

Get your stats or someone else's stats. Voting ratio, Amount of questions answered, and more in the future this feature is currently WIP and is subject to change in the future.
it is currently not possible to set your profile private.
